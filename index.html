Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

“Missile Man of India”

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He is remembered for his pivotal role in India’s missile and nuclear weapons programs, and for being an inspiring teacher, visionary, and humble leader.

Achievements

  • Known as the “Missile Man of India”.
  • Played a leading role in India’s 1998 nuclear tests.
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007).
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna in 1997.
  • Authored inspirational books like "Wings of Fire".
  • Died while delivering a lecture – a true teacher till the end.
